User Level

Beginner

getting started?
No problem! These exercises are designed for newcomers to build a strong foundation safely and effectively.

  • Simple movements with easy instructions
  • Focus on form, control, and consistency
  • No heavy weights or complex gear needed
  • Perfect for home or basic gym setups
Intermediate

Ready to level up?
You’ve got the basics down — now it’s time to push harder and grow stronger.

  • Compound movements & muscle isolation
  • Incorporates weights and machines
  • More reps, more intensity
  • Ideal for people with 3–12 months of consistent training
Advanced

Beast mode: ON.
You’re experienced, disciplined, and hungry for gains — time to go all in

  • Heavy lifting, super sets, and advanced techniques
  • Focused muscle targeting and progressive overload
  • Requires proper equipment and solid form
  • Best for 1+ years of consistent training
